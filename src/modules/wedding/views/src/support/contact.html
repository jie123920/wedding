Bycouturier is a wedding & bridesmaid dress and accessory brand for unique and lovely women that deserve dainty looks. Based in London, Bycouturier offers items developed by an in-house team of professional designers that are dedicated to feminine beauty. At Bycouturier, we believe in providing high-quality, exquisite handmade gowns that will bring out the best of you on your wedding day. Gowns are made from delicate fabrics and embellished with gorgeous embroidery. They will exceed your wildest expectations. Besides the measurements, we also take the dress styles of your locality into account. The fancy experience is worth all the efforts. Thanks to our online business model, you can purchase a gown from us for the cost of renting one from a bridal studio.
